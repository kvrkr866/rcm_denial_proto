TITLE: Timely Filing Appeal Procedure
SOURCE: SOP-TF-001
CARC_CODES: 29
PAYER_IDS: ALL

OVERVIEW:
Timely filing denials (CARC 29) occur when a claim is submitted after the payer's filing deadline.
Most commercial payers require submission within 90-365 days of the date of service.
Medicare requires submission within 12 months (1 calendar year) of the date of service.

STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE:

1. VERIFY ORIGINAL SUBMISSION DATE
   - Pull clearinghouse transmission report showing original submission timestamp
   - Check electronic remittance advice (ERA) or 277 acknowledgment for initial receipt date
   - Review billing system claim history log

2. GATHER PROOF OF TIMELY FILING
   Acceptable proof includes:
   - Electronic clearinghouse transmission confirmation report with date/time stamp
   - 277 Claim Acknowledgment with payer receipt date
   - Certified mail receipt if submitted by paper
   - 835 Electronic Remittance Advice showing prior adjudication

3. PREPARE APPEAL DOCUMENTATION
   Required documents:
   - Appeal letter (see template TEMP-TF-001)
   - Proof of timely filing (from step 2)
   - Copy of original denied claim (UB-04 or CMS-1500)
   - Copy of original EOB

4. SUBMIT APPEAL
   - Submit within payer's appeal deadline (typically 60-180 days from denial)
   - Address to payer appeals department per contract
   - Keep copy of all submitted materials
   - Log submission in billing system with tracking number

5. IF NO PROOF AVAILABLE
   - Escalate to billing supervisor for write-off determination
   - Document reason for late filing in claim notes
   - Consider preventive measures: set filing deadline alerts in billing system

KEY DEADLINES:
- Medicare: 12 months from DOS for initial claim; 120 days from denial for redetermination
- BCBS: 365 days from DOS; 180 days from denial for Level 1 appeal
- Aetna: 180 days from DOS for out-of-network; 180 days from denial for appeal
- Cigna: 180 days from DOS; 180 days from denial for appeal

PREVENTION:
- Configure billing system alerts at 60-day and 90-day filing milestones
- Run weekly unbilled claims aging report
- Assign filing deadline tracking to dedicated staff
